SKILLS

Adobe Premier Pro

Adobe premiere pro was first introduced to me this year, in the second year of my creative digital media course during a documentary film making module. I have enjoyed learning this program and using it to edit and create different videos. I am currently working on a documentary about an “interesting person”. For this I interviewed my cousin Siobhan who is a fitness influencer from Sowrds living in Bali and working online. Because of the current situation this had to be filmed over zoom, so my challenge was to use B roll and editing to make the interview interesting for the viewer. I interviewed her for an hour and got the footage down to seven minutes. After working on this documentary assignment I am feeling more confident with premier pro and I am excited to use it again to create something.

Adobe Audition

Adobe audition was first introduced to me this year, in the second year of my creative digital media course during a digital audio module. I have enjoyed learning this program and using it to edit and create different audio. I have used the skills learned in my digital audio module to create a project for a group project module. We decided to create a podcast for our project and used the audio skills and adobe audition skills to do it. I have really noticed myself improve in this module the difference between my first assignment and most recent shows how much iv improved. I also learned a lot about audio and sound that I can implement into other assignments and projects in the future. I am currently working on a radio interview style piece for this module.
